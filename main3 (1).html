How It Works

Our simple three-step process makes legal compliance accessible to everyone

1

Upload Your Contract

Simply upload your contract document in PDF, JPG, or PNG format. Our system uses OCR technology to extract text from images and scanned documents.

2

Paste the text of the new regulation or select from our database of recent Armenian laws. Our system stays updated with the latest legal changes.
